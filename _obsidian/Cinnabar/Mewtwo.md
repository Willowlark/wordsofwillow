##### Moves

###### Psychic
The user's telekinetic force is enough to cause severe harm to their target. This telekinesis is not subtle.
**Type** Psychic -- Special
**Target** Foe -- **Power** 3
**Dmg Dice** SPECIAL + 3 = 11 (+1 STAB)
**Acc Dice** DEXTERITY + CHANNEL = (12)
**Effect** Roll 1 Chance Dice to Reduce the foe's Sp. Defense.

###### Psystrike
Through sheer psychic power, the user materializes its energy to deal a heavy strike to its foe.
**Type** Psychic -- Special
**Target** Foe -- **Power** 4
**Dmg Dice** SPECIAL + 4 = 12 (+1 STAB)
**Acc Dice** DEXTERITY + CHANNEL = (12)
**Effect** This Move is resisted with the Foe's Defense instead of its Sp. Defense

###### Barrier
The user casts a protective field around itself.
**Type** Psychic -- Support
**Target** Foe -- **Power** 0
**Dmg Dice** None
**Acc Dice** SPECIAL + CHANNEL = (13)
**Effect** Increase the User's Defense by 2.

###### Self Destruct
The Pokemon makes use of its ability to blow up parts of its own body in an impressive display of power.
**Type** Normal -- Physical
**Target** Area -- **Power** 8
**Dmg Dice** STRENGTH + 8 = 14
**Acc Dice** STRENGTH + BRAWL = (10)
**Effect** After performing the move, the user receives damage equal to its remaining HP.

###### Me First
The user anticipates the next attack so it can hit the target with an astounding speed.
**Type** Normal -- Support
**Target** Foe -- **Power** 0
**Dmg Dice** None
**Acc Dice** DEXTERITY + BRAWL = (11)
**Effect** Priority 1. The User copies and uses the Move the target was intending to use. It must be a damaging Move.

###### Confusion
The target's mind is hit by a weak psychic force that leaves them wondering if they were hit by an invisible enemy. Sometimes the foe is left seeing things that aren't really there.
**Type** Psychic -- Special
**Target** Foe -- **Power** 2
**Dmg Dice** SPECIAL + 2 = 10 (+1 STAB)
**Acc Dice** DEXTERITY + CHANNEL = (12)
**Effect** Roll 1 Chance Dice to Confuse the foe.

###### Power Swap
The Pokemon employs its psychic power to make the opponent feel like it's got your weaknesses and make the user feel like it's got the target's strength.
**Type** Psychic -- Support
**Target** Foe -- **Power** 0
**Dmg Dice** None
**Acc Dice** SPECIAL + CHANNEL = (13)
**Effect** Switch your Strength and Special with your foe.
